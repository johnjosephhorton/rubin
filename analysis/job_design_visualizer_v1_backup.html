Job Design Visualizer

Steps → tasks (with AI chains) → jobs. Solves the firm's long-term cost-minimization problem.

Steps

Each step si is the atomic unit of production. Choose its execution mode: Manual (worker alone), Augmented (AI does it, human reviews; ends a chain), Automated (AI alone — must be followed by another Automated or Augmented step in the same chain). Hand-off time tHi applies if step si is the final step of a non-final job.

Derived tasks

Manual steps each become a single-step task; consecutive (Automated…, Augmented) steps form one AI-chain task. Effective skill of a chain = cAr (last step); effective time = tAr / Πi∈chain qi.

Current job partition

Each job is assigned to one worker. Click between tasks to toggle a job boundary. Red ‖ = job boundary; gray | = same job.
